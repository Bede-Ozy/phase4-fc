Positional Validations

Defenders & GKs

The Fortress

Defenders are rewarded heavily for contributing to the attack, as these actions are rarer and high-impact.

Goal Scored
+3 pts
Assist
+3 pts
Example

Dums (Defender) plays a match, scores 1 goal, and gets 1 assist.
Determined as: 1 (App) + 3 (Goal) + 3 (Assist) = 7 Total Points

Midfielders

The Engine

Balanced scoring for the playmakers who control the flow of the game.

Goal Scored
+2 pts
Assist
+2 pts
Example

Biggy (Midfielder) has an amazing game with 1 goal and 2 assists.
Determined as: 1 (App) + 2 (Goal) + 4 (2 Assists) = 7 Total Points

Forwards

The Finishers

Expected to score. Volume is key for forwards to rack up big points.

Goal Scored
+2 pts
Assist
+1 pt
Example

Drogba (Forward) scores 2 goals in a match.
Determined as: 1 (App) + 4 (2 Goals) = 5 Total Points
